\chapter*{Appendix A: Example Clinical Simulation Transcript}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Appendix A: Example Clinical Simulation Transcript}

This appendix provides a representative example of a multi-turn consultation generated by the enhanced framework. The system actively captures the latent differential diagnosis array from the \texttt{doctor\_reflection} node before each generated utterance.

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{ttfamily}
\noindent \textbf{Doctor (Turn 1):} Hello. I understand you are experiencing some chest pain and shortness of breath. Could you describe what the pain feels like, and when it started?

\vspace{0.2cm}
\noindent \textbf{Patient:} It started suddenly about an hour ago. It feels like a very heavy weight pressing down on my chest, and it's making it quite hard to catch my breath. I'm feeling very anxious.

\vspace{0.3cm}
\noindent \textbf{[doctor\_reflection JSON]}\\
{[}"Acute Myocardial Infarction", "Pulmonary Embolism", "Pneumothorax"{]}

\vspace{0.3cm}
\noindent \textbf{Doctor (Turn 2):} Has the pain spread anywhere else, like your arm, neck, or jaw? Also, do you have any other symptoms like nausea, dizziness, or sweating?

\vspace{0.2cm}
\noindent \textbf{Patient:} Yes, my left arm aches a bit, and I feel quite sweaty and slightly sick to my stomach. No dizziness though.

\vspace{0.3cm}
\noindent \textbf{Doctor (Turn 3):} REQUEST TEST: vital signs, 12-lead ECG, troponin levels.

\vspace{0.2cm}
\noindent \textbf{[measurement\_node]:} BP: 155/90, HR: 110 bpm, O2 Sat: 96\% on room air. 
ECG: ST elevation in leads II, III, aVF. 
Troponin I: Elevated (1.5 ng/mL).

\vspace{0.3cm}
\noindent \textbf{[doctor\_reflection JSON]}\\
{[}"Acute Inferior Myocardial Infarction", "Pericarditis", "Aortic Dissection"{]}

\vspace{0.3cm}
\noindent \textbf{Doctor (Turn 4):} DIAGNOSIS\_READY: Acute Inferior Myocardial Infarction.

\vspace{0.2cm}
\noindent \textbf{[moderator\_node]:} Ground Truth: Acute Myocardial Infarction. Semantic Equivalence: YES.
\end{ttfamily}
